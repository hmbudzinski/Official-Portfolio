My Experience

My name is Hayley Budzinski. I was born and raised in Anchorage, Alaska, and went to college at Colorado State University where I studied International Studies and International Development.

After College, I traveled around Europe for eight months and worked in hostels and pubs. Following that trip, I went back to Alaska for six months and worked in a restaurant to save up some money before moving to Dublin, Ireland for a year.

After living in Ireland, I moved to Denver and got a job as a Paralegal. Once I realized that being a paralegal was not what I wanted to do, I decided to take this course to change my life and become a coder!
